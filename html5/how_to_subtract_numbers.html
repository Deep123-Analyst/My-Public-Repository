How to Subtract Numbers

Here you will find instructions about the subtraction feature of the Calc App.

To successfully subtract numbers, you must know what is substarction.
  1. Enter the first number
  2. Choose the minus (-) button
  3. Enter the second number
  4. Choose the equal (=) button
The substraction result is displayed

You want to subtract 2 from 3.. The equation will look like, 3-2=1. You will enter 3, click on minus (-) and then enter 2. Then click on equal (=).

The result 1 appears on the screen.
