How to Sum-Up

Here you will find the instructions about the adding feature of the Calc App.

To successfully add numbers, you must know what is addition.
  1. Enter the first number
  2. Choose the addition (+) button
  3. Enter the second number
  4. Choose the equal (=) sign
The addition result is displayed.

You want to add 3 with 4. The equation will look like, 3+4=7. You will enter 3, click on the plus (+) sign, then enter 4 and click on equal (=) sign.

The result 7 will appear on screen.
