How to Sum-Up

Here you will find the instructions about the adding feature of the Calc App.

To successfully add numbers, you must know what is addition.
  1. Enter the first number
  2. Choose the addition (+) button
  3. Enter the second number
  4. Choose the equal (=) sign
The addition result is displayed.

You want to add 3 with 4. The equation will look like, 3+4=7. You will enter 3, click on the plus (+) sign, then enter 4 and click on equal (=) sign.

The result 7 will appear on screen.

Subtract

What is Subtraction of numbers ?

Here you will learn what is Subtraction.

To successfully perform the task of subtracting numbers, you must know what is subtraction. The term Subtraction represents a mathematical operation. It is a process, an action that you do using numbers. Subtraction allows you to find the differences of 2 numbers.

You use the minus (-) button to express the subtraction operation in the app.

In the subtraction process, you take a number and reduce it to a samller number. That is, we take away one number from another.

How to Subtract Numbers

Here you will find instructions about the subtraction feature of the Calc App.

To successfully subtract numbers, you must know what is substarction.
  1. Enter the first number
  2. Choose the minus (-) button
  3. Enter the second number
  4. Choose the equal (=) button
The substraction result is displayed

You want to subtract 2 from 3.. The equation will look like, 3-2=1. You will enter 3, click on minus (-) and then enter 2. Then click on equal (=).

The result 1 appears on the screen.
